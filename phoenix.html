EVERY LAYER.
OPTIMIZED.

We don't install bloatware or run a generic script. Every tuning session is hardware-specific — because your RTX 4090 rig deserves different treatment than a budget build.

01
Input Lag Elimination
OS timer resolution, GPU scheduling, DPC latency reduction — we cut the chain between your hand and the screen down to single-digit milliseconds.
02
BIOS Deep Dive
Most PCs ship with factory-safe defaults that throttle performance. We unlock power limits, tune C-states, and configure XMP/EXPO properly.
03
RAM Timing Optimization
Tight primary and secondary timings mean lower latency across the entire memory subsystem — critical for 1% lows in fast-paced titles.
04
Windows Kernel Tweaks
CPU affinity, interrupt steering, HPET configuration, background process elimination — the OS stack gets the same surgical attention as the hardware.
05
GPU Driver Configuration
Per-game NVIDIA or AMD settings, shader caching strategy, power profile tuning, and frame pacing configuration for maximum output.
06
Thermal Management
Sustained performance requires sustained thermals. Fan curves, thermal compound assessment, and voltage efficiency tuning to keep clocks locked under load.

// FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

Is this safe for my PC?
Every session starts with a restore point. All changes are documented and 100% reversible. We've tuned over 8,000 machines without a single data loss incident.
How much FPS will I gain?
Most clients see 30–70% average FPS improvements. Gains are hardware-dependent — older CPUs typically benefit more from OS-level work while newer builds gain more from BIOS tuning.
What games do you optimize for?
Fortnite, Valorant, CS2, Warzone, Apex Legends, Rust, EFT, and more. Tell us your main titles during booking and we'll configure game-specific profiles.
Will this void my warranty?
BIOS and OS tweaks don't void hardware warranties. If you opt for overclocking (Elite tier), we'll discuss warranty implications before making any changes.
How long does a session take?
Performance tier is ~90 minutes. Competitive runs 2–2.5 hours. Elite sessions can take up to 4 hours with full stability testing included.
Do I need anything special?
Just AnyDesk or TeamViewer installed, a stable internet connection, and 30 minutes before the session to close all active applications and games.
